2017 Southeast Asain Games

The 2017 Sountheast Asian Games (Malay:Asia Tenggara 2017),officially knowns as the the 29th Southeast Asian Games(Malay:Sukan Asia Tenggara ke-29) and commonly known as Kuala Lumpur 2017 is a regional multi-sport event that will take place in Kuala Lumpur,Malaysia from 19 to 31 August 2017, with 405 events in 38 sports to be featured in the games.


It will be the sixth time that the Southeasy Asian Games held in Malaysia, after 1965,1971,1989 and 2001 Games and its first since 2001


By the time the country hosts the games in 2017, it will be 16 years after Malaysian last hostes the biennial even. The Games closing ceremoney will concide with Malaysian's 60the Independence Day.
